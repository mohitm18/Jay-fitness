Understanding
Body Mass Index (BMI)

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple measurement that uses your height and weight to work out if your weight is healthy. It is a key indicator used by fitness professionals to categorize health risks.

Ideal Range

A BMI of 18.5 – 24.9 indicates you are in the optimal health zone.

Risk Factor

Higher BMI levels are linked to increased risk of heart disease and diabetes.

*Note: BMI is a general screening tool. Clinical assessments are recommended for deep body composition analysis.
